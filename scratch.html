Last 5 winners of the Masters

  1. Rory Mcllroy

    • McIlroy's victory at the 2025 Masters completed his career Grand Slam, making him the 6th golfer to win all four major championships


  2. Scottie Sceffler (2X)

    • Scheffler secured his second Masters title in 2024, having previously won in 2022, showcasing his consistent performance at Augusta National


  3. Jon Rahm

    • Rahm became the fourth Spanish golfer to win the Masters, following in the footsteps of legends like Seve Ballesteros, José María Olazábal, and Sergio García


  4. Hideki Matsuyama

    • Matsuyama made history as the first Japanese male golfer to win a major championship, bringing immense pride to his home country
